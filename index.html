Welcome To Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock!

Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock is an extended version of the classic game Rock, Paper, Scissors that adds two more options: Lizard and Spock. This makes the game more exciting and reduces the chances of a tie!

Go head to head against the computer and see if you have what it takes to defeat machine itself!

Challenge friend or foe in an intense one on one game!
